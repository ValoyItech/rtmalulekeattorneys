Our Legal Services

We offer comprehensive legal services tailored to meet our clients' diverse needs.

Criminal Law & Bail Applications

Expert representation in criminal matters, including bail applications, plea bargaining, and trial defense.

Civil Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Representation in civil disputes, including contract disputes, debt collection, and alternative dispute resolution.

Divorce & Family Law

Assistance with divorce proceedings, maintenance, custody matters, and domestic violence cases.

Wills, Estates & Trusts

Drafting of wills, estate planning, administration of deceased estates, and trust formation.

Road Accident Fund (RAF) Claims

Property Transfers & Conveyancing

Handling of property transfers, bond registrations, and all conveyancing matters.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Company formations, corporate governance, compliance, and commercial transactions.

Contract Drafting & Review

Drafting, reviewing and vetting of various contracts and agreements.

Notarial Services & Antenuptial Contracts

Notarization of documents and drafting of antenuptial contracts.
